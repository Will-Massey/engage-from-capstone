## FAQ (short)

**Is Engage only for proposals?**  
Yes — we're proposal-first. Deeper practice management stays in AccountFlow, Xero, or your existing stack.

**Does Clara send emails without my approval?**  
Never. Clara drafts; you approve every client-facing email before it goes out.

**Is the e-sign legally defensible in the UK?**  
Engage records simple electronic signatures with full forensic metadata. Enterprise tier will offer qualified e-sign (DocuSign) for firms that require it.

**Can I import my service catalog?**  
Yes — CSV bulk import is available on all tiers.
